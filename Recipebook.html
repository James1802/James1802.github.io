Chicken mushroom Risotto

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 4
Quantity Ingredients
3 chicken thigh fillets shredded
1 large red onion finely chopped
2 portobello mushrooms
2 cups ckicken stock
1/2 cup Aborio rice
2 cloves garlic
5 sprigs thyme finely chopped
for pan oil for pan
125g parmesan finely grated

Method

Step 1: Put the onion and garlic in a pan over medium high heat with a generous amount of olive oil.

Step 2: Once onion is trnaslucent add the chicken and cook till brown. Then transfer to a pot and add the 1/2 cup of the chicken stock. Once the stock is in yo can add the mushrooms and the rice and turn down to medium low heat.

Step 3: Once stock reduced add the thyme and another 1/2 a cup of stock to the pot and let simmer unitl it's absorbed into the rice then contine this proccess until you use all the stock.

Step 4: Once the remaining stock has absorbed into the rice to make it a sloppy but firm consistency take the pot off the heat and stir in 3/4 of the parmesan to make it creamy.

Step 5: Serve with remaining parmesan.
